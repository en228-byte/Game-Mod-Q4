Quake 4 Mods for IT266

This mod is based on the anime Blue Lock during the second selection where teams of 2 face off and either steal or lose a teammate where the winners go to a 3v3, 4v4, then 5v5. It is best to pick up players based on their stats to fit what your team need. Currently in this mod you can kick the ball around to score points. You must hit a goal exactly to be awarded a point to the corresponding side and reset the ball.

Instructions:
-Go to the pause menu/settings and press the Blue Lock button to see the in game help screen
-Press H to activate the player recruit screen where you can see player stats (or steal a player after winning a match - to be implemented)
-kick the ball towards either goal using WASD, if the ball hits a goal the corresponding side will get a point (displayed at the top of the screen) and the ball will go back to the middle of the field.
